M.Sc. in Computer Science Student

EPFL

Hello there! I am Edoardo, a Computer Science M.Sc. student at EPFL, with a strong focus on Machine Learning and Security. I earned a Computer Engineering B.Sc. at PoliTo, after spending my sophomore year as an exchange student at Tongji University (同济大学) in Shanghai, supported by PoliTong scholarship.

My research interest is Trustworthy ML in its different aspects.

I am currently working on my Master thesis about the robustness ov Vision Transformers in Prof. Mittal’s lab at Princeton University, and I am one of the co-authors and maintainers of RobustBench, a standardized benchmark for adversarial robustness.

Last summer I worked as a Software Engineering Intern at Bloomberg LP, and previously, during the Fall 2020 semester, I was a Research Intern at armasuisse CYD Campus, supervised by Dr. Humbert.

During the Spring 2020 semester, I did a research project at Prof. Troncoso SPRING Lab, where I worked on influence functions with the aim of counteracting deepfakes.

I attended Military Navy College “F. Morosini”, in Venice, I am a member of LeadTheFuture, the leading mentoring program for STEM students in Italy, and I have been junior entrepreneur at JEToP.

I love swimming, sailing, and travelling.
